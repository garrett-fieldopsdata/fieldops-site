How it works

From lead to paid — four steps.

A full loop around a job takes 60 seconds of tracking. Here's what it looks like.

1

Log the lead

Hit n — name the job, pick the customer. Stage: Lead.

2

Quote + drag

Drop your quote amount. Drag the card to Quoted. Follow up on the auto-task.

3

Run the job

Drag to Active. Upload photos from the field. Log cost + hours.

4

Invoice → Paid

Send the invoice (from the RigStarter template). Drag to Paid when the ACH hits.

FAQ

Honest answers.

Is my data in the cloud?
Your data syncs to secure cloud storage tied to your account, so it's available on any device you log into. You can also export a local JSON backup anytime from Settings.
What if I switch phones or clear my browser?
Export a backup first (Settings → Export). The file contains all your contacts, jobs, tasks, and photos as a single JSON. On the new device, click Import and you're back in business. We recommend exporting weekly — takes 2 seconds.
Does it work offline? Really?
Yep. Open the app once with signal, then you can run the whole thing from a pad on no-service lease road. Your changes stay local. Next time you're online, export a backup so it's safe.
Do I need an account?
Yes — a free account keeps your data synced across devices and backed up to the cloud. Sign up takes 60 seconds and requires an access code during beta.
Can I import from a spreadsheet?
V1.1 — spreadsheet import is on the roadmap. For now, adding a few dozen contacts takes about 10 minutes. If you're coming from the RigStarter Bundle's Job Tracker, we'll ship a direct importer first.
How is this different from RigStarter?
RigStarter is the spreadsheet system — invoices, estimates, job tracker. Perfect when you're running 1–3 jobs a month. Field Ops Data is what you graduate to when the spreadsheet can't keep up and you want a real pipeline + photo storage. Bundle buyers get first access.
Mobile app?
The site works great on mobile — add it to your home screen (Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen, Chrome → Menu → Install app) and it runs like a native app. Native iOS/Android apps on the Crew roadmap.
What if it breaks?
Email support@fieldopsdata.com. During beta I reply personally — usually within an hour during daylight. Bug reports get fixed fast. Feature requests go on the roadmap.
